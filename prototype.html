Where to Roam

High-fidelity Prototype

Following the presentation of our low-fidelity prototypes to the class and clients, we gathered several improvement suggestions, summarized below:

  • Consolidate every option over to the left bar side of the screen.
  • Remove the infographic option if not enough variables are selected.
  • Add a way to be able to visualize two different types of the data when comparing. i.e one community bar graph compared to another community line graph.
  • Label text field in the search bar on the homepage for accessibility purposes.
After evaluating and integrating the majority of the feedback into our design, we proceeded to develop a high-fidelity prototype. High-fidelity prototypes are detailed and interactive representations of the final product. We selected Figma for creating our high-fidelity prototype due to its robust design and collaboration features. Below are some screenshots from our high-fidelity prototype, showcasing various aspects of the design. They illustrate the user interface and functionality. Next, we will focus on refining the prototype based on further testing and feedback.
